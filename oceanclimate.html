We are tied to the ocean. And when we go back to the sea - whether it is to sail or to watch it - we are going back from whence we came. ~ John F. Kennedy

Current Research

I am finishing up work on determining the anthropogenic CO2 in the northern South China Sea basin. It is a comprehensive investigation that includes high-resolution spatiotemporal variations throughout the water column.

Recently, I am transitioning my foci to the U.S. East Coast, where I will examine terrestrial and ocean margin carbon export fluxes to the open ocean as well as the anthroopgenic CO2 propagations in this region.
