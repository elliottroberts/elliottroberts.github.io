About me

I am a Welsh-American, with a Ph.D in Marine Chemistry from Xiamen University, China.
Currently, I am a post-doctoral researcher at the University of Delaware, U.S.A, where we examine the coastal ocean carbon dynamics along the U.S. East Coast. In China, I primarily examined the ocean CO2 carbonate system and anthropogenic CO2 in the northern South China Sea basin.

Experience

Scientist with 12+ years’ experience in oceanographic and climate science research. Published author & coauthor with four peer reviewed publications, one first-author publication in progress, and presentations in U.S.A., France, China, and Singapore. Excellent writing and communication skills; fluent in English and Chinese. Capable of using new and existing data in novel research to investigate broad range of topics while controlling data quality and documenting progress. Technical proficiency using Python, R, MATLAB, SQL, Surfer, Ocean Data View, IBM Watson Studio, and Microsoft Office.
