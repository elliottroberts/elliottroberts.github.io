Diversity, equity, and inclusivity

Diversity, equity, and inclusion are notions that influenced me immensely since I lived in six countries, so these thoughts were critical to be more receptive to different cultures and practices. It is considered a necessity to be mindful of certain groups that have been marginalized and view individuals on equal footing, regardless of individual or generalized group characteristics. Incorporation of these notions and the consistent practice of them fosters increased understanding, insight, and harmony between the practitioner and the interacted individual (or group).

I actively incorporate the above notions not only on a team level, but individuals within my sphere of influence. I have teaching experience with students with disadvantaged backgrounds, disabilities, and affected by war (specifically, the Ambazonia War)
