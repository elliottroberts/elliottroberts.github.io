Research Interests

I am interested in marine carbon dioxide removal (mCDR), particularly using coastal enhanced weathering to sequester CO2 from the atmosphere to the ocean. As this involves changes in the coastal ocean system, I am interested in oceanic CO2-carbonate chemistry and marine productivity in this complex system - their dynamisms and how they evolve with increasing anthropogenic influence.
